About Me

Hey! My name is Atharv Kale and I am the founder of Atharv Photography, which I manage myself. I'm a sophomore in high school and have a passion for photography, chess, tennis, and basketball. I love using a camera and want to bring those precious moments to you.

I have over 8 years of experience, from portrait to nature to city photography, and I can guide you for amazing and exquisite moments.

Photography isn’t just a hobby; it’s a way of seeing the world. I combine patience, creativity, and technical skills to craft each image, ensuring that each moment feels timeless.
