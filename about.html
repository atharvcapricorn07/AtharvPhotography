About Me

Hi, I’m Atharv Kale, the creator of Atharv Photography. I’m currently a high school sophomore with a passion for capturing stories through the lens. Beyond photography, I enjoy chess, tennis, and basketball—but nothing excites me more than freezing moments in time and sharing them with others.

With over 8 years of experience spanning portraits, nature, and city photography, I’ve had the opportunity to explore a wide range of styles. My goal is to help you create photos that feel both natural and unforgettable.

To me, photography isn’t just about taking pictures—it’s about capturing the feeling behind them. Every shoot is a chance to create something meaningful, and I can’t wait to keep learning, experimenting, and sharing my work with others.
